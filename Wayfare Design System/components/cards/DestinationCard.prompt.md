The hero of the system — a full-bleed photographic card with a bottom protection gradient and overlaid serif caption. Use in destination grids and "most popular" rails.

```jsx
<DestinationCard
  image="/photos/maldives.jpg"
  region="Indian Ocean"
  title="The Maldives"
  price="$1,240 / night"
  rating="4.9"
  badge="Overwater"
/>
```

Image hovers with a slow zoom. Omit `image` for a tonal placeholder labelled "Your photo". Props: `region`, `title`, `meta`, `price`, `rating`, `badge`, `height`, `onClick`.
